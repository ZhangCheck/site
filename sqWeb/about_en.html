3Qii is an international internet company specializes in cross-border electronic payment, cross-border content publishing and internet products design. We’re dedicated to integrate global resources, create cross-border channel league and provide a international channel platform for Chinese CP.

The headquarter is set in Hong Kong and there are many offices scattered all over the world. Our services are centered Hong Kong and cover China and Southeast Asia (Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Philippines and Singapore) and will include Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Egypt), Latin America (Mexico, Brazil, Chile, Argentina), Russia, and Africa (Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya).

3Qii has established a favorable cooperation relationship with local operators and channels in multiple countries and regions. We inherit and uphold the notion of being the bridge while providing superior systematic integrated services for Chinese Content Providers.

Four Product Lines

3Payer provides safe and fast cross-border electronic payment service which is a one-stop service that combined with multiple payment channels. We provide CP with multiple payment platforms such as SDK, API, which can apply on Android mobile games, website games and online games and so forth.

We have a professional international publishing team, which lays the way for Chinese Content Providers to participate globally based on enormous data pool of user behavior and habits so that the content can be published in specific regions aimed at exact gamers via cross-border regional channel traffic.

We have local channels of a lot of countries and devote ourselves to provide Content Providers with a globalized channel network so as to maximize revenue. With global resources, we can provide online and offline multiple channels, which support CPS/CPA/CPC/CPI and other modes.

As an A-class company of internet products design in China, our internet products and software with user centered design can provide interactive experience and perfect design of interface, which definitely can make your product alive.
