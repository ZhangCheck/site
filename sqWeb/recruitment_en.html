User Experience Designer

Responsibilities

  • 1. Profound understanding of development of e-commence industry and product planning;
  • 2. Framework design of users’ behaviors and product information, guarantee the availability of product;
  • 3. Participate the planning and design of e-commence products, summarize users’ goals and tasks;
  • 4. Take part in user research, and optimize the design plan according to the results of it;
  • 5. Broach improvement plan for the availability testing and assessment of existing products, constantly optimize user experience of products;
  • 6. Join the formulation and implementation of interface specifications;
  • 7. Organize and build a team of user experience and cultivate the greener.

Requirements

  • 1. Above Bachelor’s degree, relevant majors of industrial design, computer, psychology, College English Test Brand 4;
  • 2. More than 3 years of working experience of interactive design in website and software industry with successful cases;

  • 4. Profound experience and understanding of e-commerce industry;
  • 5. Provide high-quality prototype of product, flow diagram and wireframe which can demonstrate the design plan expressly;
  • 6. Proficiency of all kinds of methods of interactive design, deep understanding and cognition of theory of interactive design;
  • 7. Profound insight of up-to-date interactive applications in the industry;
  • 8. Acute grasp of user experience and availability;
  • 9. Out-going, optimistic, be good at teaming up with people who have all kind of backgrounds;
  • 10. Passionate, proactive, eyes on details, impressive learning capacity;
  • 11. Good reading ability in English;
